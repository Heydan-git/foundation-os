# Foundation OS Community Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers of Foundation OS pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

### ✅ **Encouraged Behaviors**
- **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with dignity and respect
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and help them feel part of the community
- **Be Constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together towards common goals
- **Be Patient**: Understand that people have different skill levels and backgrounds
- **Be Professional**: Maintain appropriate language and behavior
- **Give Credit**: Acknowledge others' contributions and ideas
- **Show Empathy**: Consider others' perspectives and feelings

### ❌ **Unacceptable Behaviors**
- **Harassment**: Of any kind, including unwelcome comments about personal characteristics
- **Discrimination**: Based on any protected characteristic
- **Trolling**: Deliberately provocative or inflammatory behavior
- **Personal Attacks**: Ad hominem arguments or insults
- **Doxxing**: Publishing private information without consent
- **Spam**: Irrelevant or repetitive content
- **Plagiarism**: Using others' work without proper attribution
- **Off-topic Disruption**: Derailing conversations or discussions

## Community Standards

### 💬 **Communication Guidelines**

#### In All Interactions
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

#### In Technical Discussions
- Stay on topic and contribute meaningfully
- Back up claims with evidence or reasoning
- Admit when you don't know something
- Ask clarifying questions before making assumptions
- Provide helpful context for your suggestions

#### In Code Reviews
- Focus on the code, not the person
- Provide specific, actionable feedback
- Explain the reasoning behind suggestions
- Be open to different approaches
- Recognize good work and improvements

### 🤝 **Collaboration Expectations**

#### For All Contributors
- **Read the documentation** before asking questions
- **Search existing issues** before creating new ones
- **Follow project conventions** and established patterns
- **Test your changes** before submitting
- **Write clear commit messages** and PR descriptions

#### For Maintainers
- **Respond promptly** to contributors (within 72 hours)
- **Provide clear guidance** on how to improve contributions
- **Be encouraging** to new contributors
- **Maintain consistency** in review standards
- **Document decisions** and rationale clearly

#### For Community Leaders
- **Model the behavior** you want to see
- **Address issues promptly** and fairly
- **Support diverse perspectives** and approaches
- **Foster growth** and learning opportunities
- **Maintain transparency** in decision-making

## Enforcement

### 📋 **Reporting Process**

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Direct Report**: Email conduct@foundation-os.dev
2. **Anonymous Report**: Use our [anonymous form](https://foundation-os.dev/report)
3. **Community Leader**: Contact any community leader directly
4. **Emergency**: For immediate safety concerns, contact local authorities

### 📝 **Report Information**

Please include as much of the following information as possible:
- **Who**: People involved in the incident
- **What**: Description of the unacceptable behavior
- **When**: Date and time of the incident
- **Where**: Location or platform where it occurred
- **Context**: Any relevant background information
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, or other documentation

### 🔒 **Confidentiality**

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. We will not share reporter information without explicit permission, except when required by law or to prevent harm.

### ⚖️ **Enforcement Actions**

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards. They may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

#### Level 1: Warning
- **Action**: Private written warning
- **Reason**: Minor violations or first-time issues
- **Duration**: No specific timeframe
- **Appeal**: Can be discussed with community leaders

#### Level 2: Temporary Restriction
- **Action**: Temporary ban from community spaces
- **Reason**: Repeated violations or moderate infractions
- **Duration**: 1 week to 3 months
- **Appeal**: Formal appeal process available

#### Level 3: Permanent Ban
- **Action**: Permanent exclusion from community spaces
- **Reason**: Severe violations or repeated serious infractions
- **Duration**: Permanent
- **Appeal**: Limited appeal process for exceptional circumstances

### 📊 **Transparency Report**

We publish quarterly transparency reports including:
- Number of reports received
- Types of violations reported
- Actions taken (anonymized)
- Process improvements made

## Community Leaders

Our community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct:

### 🛡️ **Responsibilities**
- **Investigate reports** fairly and thoroughly
- **Determine appropriate responses** to violations
- **Communicate decisions** clearly to all parties
- **Support affected community members**
- **Improve processes** based on feedback

### 🎯 **Selection Criteria**
- Demonstrated commitment to Foundation OS
- Strong track record of positive community participation
- Experience with conflict resolution
- Understanding of diversity and inclusion principles
- Availability to respond to reports promptly

### 📧 **Current Leaders**
For the current list of community leaders, see [community.foundation-os.dev/leaders](https://community.foundation-os.dev/leaders)

## Appeal Process

If you believe an enforcement action was unjust:

1. **Submit Appeal**: Email appeal@foundation-os.dev within 30 days
2. **Provide Context**: Explain why you believe the action was inappropriate
3. **Review Process**: Independent review by different community leaders
4. **Decision**: Final decision communicated within 14 days
5. **Implementation**: Any changes implemented immediately
